ABOUT ME

Hey there! I’m Melisha Shakya — a curious, coffee-fueled UX/UI designer, currently working on my Master’s in User Experience Design. I started out in Computer Science major, but quickly realized I was way more excited about creating things than just writing code behind the scenes. Right now, I’m diving into everything from wireframes and user flows to color theory and usability testing. I might be early in my UX journey, but I’m building a strong foundation and loving every step of the process (even the messy Figma files). When I’m not designing, I’m probably sketching, making digital art, singing badly (but confidently), or getting way too competitive over video games. I’m all about blending creativity and logic — whether it’s through a clean interface or a quick doodle.
