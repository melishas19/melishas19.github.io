TYPE PERSONA SPECIMAN

The Type Persona Speciman App was designed as a visual and interactive tool for exploring individual typefaces, one at a time. This edition focus on the classic Baskerville font family across different weights published by URW type Foundry.

TIMELINE

Mar-May 2025

PLATFORM

Mobile App

MY ROLE

UX/UI Designer

Introduction

This app serves both designers and typography enthusiasts who wish to experiment with font weights in real time on a mobile device. It allows users to engage deeper into the details in Baskerville’s font design, appearance, and readability.

Problems

There are many tools to preview fonts but most of them:
  • Lack showcasing the dynamic comparison across weights and style.
  • Provide fewer to no information on the each character’s versatility and aesthetic

Goals

  • Let users explore all weights of Baskerville font (Regular, Bold, Medium Oblique, etc.).
  • Offer customization typographic phrase.
  • Enable users to interact and learn more about different characters.

DESIGN PROCESS

Research & Type Analysis

Used the type persona guide to determine the characteristics of the Baskerville typeface. Analyzed web apps like Adobe Fonts, IBM Pictogram Viewer, and Typeface App.

UI Design

Minimalist look for easier readability and interaction having contrast between sharp shapes with rounded textiles. The color palette consists of darker purple and indigo hues which aligns perfectly with Baskerville’s classic and elegant personality. The font color varies throughout the app based on intractability, however the central focal character font is black to ensure high stroke contrast and crisp.

Wireframes

Central font page with a grid containing characters Character page previewing individual character for in-depth details. Text page to test custom words or phrases and also view preset sample phrase. Top title with navigation slider to slide between each pages. Bottom weight navigation buttons to compare various font weights.
