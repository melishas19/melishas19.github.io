TYPE PERSONA SPECIMAN

The Type Persona Speciman App was designed as a visual and interactive tool for exploring individual typefaces, one at a time and this edition focus on the classic Baskerville font family.

TIMELINE

Mar-May 2025

PLATFORM

Mobile App

MY ROLE

UX/UI Designer

SUMMARY

This app serves both designers and typography enthusiasts who wish to experiment with font weights in real time on a mobile device. It allows users to engage deeper into the details in Baskerville’s font design, appearance, and readability.

PROBLEMS

There are many tools to preview fonts but most of them:
  • Lack showcasing the dynamic comparison across weights and style.
  • Provide fewer to no information on the each character’s versatility and aesthetic

GOALS

  • Let users explore all weights of Baskerville font (Regular, Bold, Medium Oblique, etc.).
  • Offer customization typographic phrase.
  • Enable users to interact and learn more about different characters.

Research & Type Analysis

Used the type persona guide to determine the characteristics of the Baskerville typeface. Analyzed web apps like Adobe Fonts, IBM Pictogram Viewer, and Typeface App.

Sketches

UI Design

  • Minimalist look for easier readability and interaction having contrast between sharp shapes with rounded textiles.
  • The color palette consists of darker purple and indigo hues which aligns perfectly with Baskerville’s classic and elegant personality.
  • The font color varies throughout the app based on intractability, however the central focal character font is black to ensure high stroke contrast and crisp.

RESULT

  • Users could easily navigate through font weights and preview them in real-time.
  • Users can immediately sense the “persona” of the typeface.
  • A clean, focused UI that puts type at the center.

Tool Used

  • Figma for wireframe and UI design
  • Adobe Fonts/ Google Fonts API for font integrations
